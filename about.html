Our Story

It started as a room. It became a philosophy.

Stackly began in 2019 with a single studio and a stubborn belief: that the space you work in shapes the work you make. Six years later we operate a dozen locations across three cities — but the philosophy hasn't changed.

We're a small team of designers, hospitality professionals, and operators, obsessed with the details that make a workday feel considered instead of chaotic.

Values

The details are the design.

Everything in a Stackly space — from the coffee origin to the chair angle to the acoustics of the phone booths — is a decision, not a default. We treat our members' time and attention as scarce, and design accordingly.
